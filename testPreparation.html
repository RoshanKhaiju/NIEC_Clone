Test Preparation Courses in Kathmandu
Overview

Higher education is an integral part of a student’s life and pursuing higher education in abroad countries has become a popular trend. As an international student seeking to study abroad, there are various admission prerequisites to consider.

Students who wish to obtain admissions to pursue your Bachelor’s, Master’s or doctoral degrees in an international university are required to take aptitude tests like GRE, GMAT or SAT. As a student with English as your second language, it is also important that you take English Language Proficiency Tests like IELTS, TOEFL or PTE. Depending on the choice of your destination for studies and your field of study, the tests required could vary.

NIEC offers test preparation classes for SAT, GRE, GMAT, IELTS, TOEFL and PTE. Our certified and highly experienced instructors help you achieve your required scores delivering the courses in the most simplified techniques. NIEC also offers mock tests for students where our instructors follow the standard guidelines for assessment in order to advice you on your strengths and weaknesses.

Thousands of students choose to study with us every year and they have achieved great success. Join our test preparation courses and unleash your real potentials!

Our instructors

We have certified instructors who are available at any time of the day and you can simply book your classes as per your convenience. Our instructors who have years of teaching experience will guide you and work with you closely to monitor your skills. Emphasizing your weaknesses and strengths, our experts will help you brush up your skills to achieve your required scores in the test.

Let us help you make your learning experience stress-free with simplified teaching techniques focused and tailored as per your need!

Duration of Courses

The duration of the classes offered online and physically will vary depending on the courses.

The duration of classes for IELTS, PTE and TOEFL will be 6 weeks while the duration of classes for GMAT, GRE and SAT will be 7 weeks.

You will have one hour of regular classes from Monday to Friday for IELTS, TOEFL and PTE whereas you will have two hours of classes for GMAT, GRE and SAT.

Mock Tests at NIEC

We conduct weekly mock tests on every Sunday. You can opt to either take the full tests or as per the sections you have completed in your course.

The mock tests are carefully marked by our instructors following the guidelines for grading as per the real tests and your results will be easy available. Giving feedback is very crucial and our instructors will provide you with the best possible advices for improvement.

Test your skills and make progress with NIEC’s weekly mock tests!

On Demand test

We usually conduct weekly mock tests on Sunday however, for students unable to attend the tests, we also offer ‘on demand tests’ where you can request for mock tests as required. You will need to inform us in advance for the on demand tests.

Daily assignment

Learning is a continuous process and it is important that you spend extra time on your courses apart from the everyday one/two hour of classes. In order to support your classes, we provide you with daily assignments. The daily assignments are specially focused on providing you with every day practice to help you improve your skills.

Revision Classes

Although the duration of our courses is 6 to 7 weeks, we offer revision classes for our students. Your instructors who are keeping track of your progress from Week 1 will suggest you to take the revision classes as required. Or, you can simply take your revision classes if you feel you are not ready for your real test.

Booking of Tests

Once you are ready to take the real tests, we also provide you with our support to assist you in booking the dates for your real test. You can check with us the available test dates and simply register for the tests!

E-Library

You can make the best from our e-library where lots of study materials are available. The study materials will provide you with assistance and help you gather more knowledge required for the tests. We have updated and recent practice materials that will be useful to you. Check our e-library and discover all that you need to know!
